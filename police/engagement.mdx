---
title: "Engagement"
description: "General guide on how to engage with other players."
---

- You must engage with citizens professionally at all times.
- You are required to provide a supervisor when requested, if one is not available, any higher ranking officer can act as substitute. if no higher ranking officer is available, an officer of the same rank as you can substitute.

  <Info>
    If no same ranking officer is available tell them that there is no supervisor available so you cannot proceed with the request, and continue with your RP.
  </Info>
- You are not permitted to threaten or disrespect any civilians in any way.
- You are not permitted to use any reactions on discord to any form of disciplinary action with the Police Department.
