---
title: "Communication"
description: "A list of our communication guidelines."
---

- You are expected not to disrupt or interrupt communication within radio channels. Do not interrupt other officers, interrupt only if its an emergency. Do not casually converse in the RTO.
- You are expected to have the ability to hear and speak to RTO at all times when not on a scene or busy.
- You are expected to always use 10-codes and keep transmissions brief.
- You are permitted to use a Radio Account.
- You are expected to refrain from arguments, however respectful discussions are permitted.Â
