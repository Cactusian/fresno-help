description: "A list describing the priorities of life for the Fresno Police Department in Fresno Roleplay."

1. **Safety of Uninvolved Citizens**\
   Uninvolved, or otherwise innocent, citizens of the country (including emergency responders) are the top priority of our department at all times
2. **Safety of Involved citizens**\
   Involved citizens, including emergency responders, are the 2nd priority of the department. Their lives should be valued above that of the suspect.
