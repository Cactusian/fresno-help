---
title: "Supervisory Promotions"
description: "Each respective supervisory rank has a set cooldown of weeks-in-rank to be promoted to any rank above Sergeant."
---

| Rank               | Time              |
| ------------------ | ----------------- |
| SGT -\> LT         | 2 Weeks (14 Days) |
| LT -\> CPT         | 4 Weeks (28 Days) |
| CPT -\> Major      | 6 Weeks (42 Days) |
| Major -\> Colonel  | Unknown           |
| D. Chief -\> Chief | Unknown           |

<Info>
  This may be disregarded or voided by the Chief (or D. Chief) when needed.
</Info>
