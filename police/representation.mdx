---
title: "Representation"
description: "Learn how to represent the Fresno Police Department in Fresno Roleplay."
---

- All Police Department Officers are expected to maintain a professional demeanor at all times.
- You are expected to always abide by department policies whilst representing the Police Department.
- You are expected to utilize proper spelling, punctuation and grammar (SPaG)
- You are expected to remain respectful and subordinate to higher ranking department members.
- You are expected to refer to higher ranking personnel by their rank.
