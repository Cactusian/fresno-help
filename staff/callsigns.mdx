---
title: "Staff Callsigns"
description: "These are the callsigns that Staff should use while on-shift in-game. The first letter is always going to be X, followed by a 2nd letter which represents your rank, a dash and 3 numbers of choice."
---

| **Rank**       | **Callsign**    |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| Foundership    | XA-000          |
| Directors      | XB-000          |
| Management     | XC-000          |
| Administrators | XX-000          |
| Moderators     | XZ-000 / XY-000 |
